📌 Causes of Diabetes

Type 1 Diabetes is an autoimmune reaction destroying insulin-producing cells. It’s not preventable and often diagnosed in youth.

Type 2 Diabetes occurs when body becomes resistant to insulin or doesn't produce enough. Key risk factors: Excess weight, physical inactivity, family history, age >45, ethnicity.

Gestational Diabetes develops during pregnancy and raises future Type 2 diabetes risk for mother and child.

🍎 Diabetes Prevention Strategies

✅ Lose 5-7% of body weight if overweight.
✅ Eat whole grains, veggies, lean protein. Limit sugary drinks.
✅ 150+ min moderate activity weekly.
✅ Quit smoking & manage blood pressure.
✅ Prediabetes can often be reversed with lifestyle changes.
